Welcome to Campus Shoppy

The number one shopping site for Maseno University Students

Welcome to Campus Shoppy, the tailored online marketplace designed exclusively for Maseno University Students. We know student life is busy—juggling classes, studies, and social activities leaves little time for complicated shopping trips. That’s why we built a platform that puts speed and convenience first.

Our commitment is simple: we make sure that Maseno students can buy goods and pay for them easily.Campus Shoppy solves the common pain points of campus commerce by providing:

Easy Buying: Browse a curated collection of products relevant to the Maseno community, including books, electronics, fashion, and essentials.Our interface is fast and simple to navigate.

Fast Effortless Payment: We integrate payment methods that are designed for speed and accessibility, ensuring transactions are completed smoothly every time.

Community Focus: This is a platform for students, by the community. We help connect buyers with trusted sellers right here on campus, fostering a supportive local economy.
